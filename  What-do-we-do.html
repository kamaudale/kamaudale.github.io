CARTOGRAPHY

This is the study of creating maps. As 
cartographers we come in different forms performing different tasks. For example, it could be softcopy mapping, web mapping or digital maps. For these mapping products, you may have to suggest design and quality control/assurance.






DATABASE MANAGEMENT

We perform storing vector and raster data in a relational database management system. Because database can grow exponentially in size, you may have a role in database development, database administration or any type of asset management consulting.

SPATIAL ANALYSIS

 With different type of techniques we manipulate, extract, locate and analyze geographic data. Through the use of geoprocessing tools and data analysis to explore the relationships between map feature. Often, we  develop workflow and GIS modeling techniques for normal business operations. Furthermore, through automating these redundant GIS processes, we perform programming in a GIS analysis.

REMOTE SENSING:

Using satellite data as  a company we classify land cover. Even if you don’t use remote sensing, you probably will do air photo interpretation or use global positioning systems (GPS).  

SURVEYING and BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT 

Finally, as  Surveyors we accurately measure three-dimensional points on the land. Business development involves project management, technical writing and management.
